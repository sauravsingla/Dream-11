# Dream11 Fantasy Cricket Team Optimizer

This repository provides an open-source Python implementation for **Dream11 fantasy cricket team selection** using **binary integer programming** and **risk-aware optimisation**.

It is designed for researchers, students and practitioners searching for work on:

- Dream11 team prediction and Dream 11 team selection;
- fantasy cricket optimisation and fantasy sports analytics;
- cricket player selection and constrained squad optimisation;
- integer programming, mixed-integer programming and operations research;
- PuLP, CBC and Gurobi optimisation models in Python;
- Markowitz-inspired risk modelling for sports analytics;
- captain and vice-captain optimisation under roster and credit constraints.

## Problem addressed

The model selects exactly eleven cricket players while satisfying fantasy-sports roster rules, player-category limits, team limits, a credit budget, and captain and vice-captain assignments. Expected fantasy score is balanced against player-performance variability through a configurable risk-aversion coefficient.

## Research publication

The implementation accompanies the paper **“Integer Optimisation for Dream 11 Cricket Team Selection”** by Saurav Singla and Swapna Samir Shukla, published in the *International Journal of Computer Sciences and Engineering* in November 2020.

DOI: `10.26438/ijcse/v8i11.16`
